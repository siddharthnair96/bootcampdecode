About me

I am from India and am 25 years old. I have stayed and studyed in Mumbai most my life. After completing my undergrad I started of with an internship for a year in Dubai and then joined the Management Training Program to pre-open a hotel. After completing my course here at Les Roches I plan to join an organization leads or paves the way for the digital realm for hospitality.
